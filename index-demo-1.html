Cubic3D App

Type

In-Vehicle Advertising

Date

23.05.2024

Role

UI/UX Designer

Client

Qubic3D

Qubic3D is transforming rideshare advertising by integrating static posters and interactive tablet displays into vehicles. This project focuses on providing engaging, geo-targeted ad experiences while offering drivers an opportunity to earn passive income. My role was to design an intuitive UI/UX experience for both advertisers and drivers, ensuring a seamless interaction between brands and passengers.

The challenge

Traditional in-vehicle advertising often lacks engagement, interactivity, and precise targeting. The goal was to create a modern, digital-forward solution that enhances brand visibility while keeping passengers entertained and drivers incentivized. The system needed to be scalable, allowing for a smooth transition from static posters to fully interactive touchscreen displays.

My solution

I designed an intuitive ad display system that combines static posters, interactive ads, and gamified content to enhance rider engagement. I also developed a driver dashboard, allowing seamless ad management, earnings tracking, and analytics access. By integrating QR codes, geo-targeting, and performance tracking, the platform offers advertisers real-time insights and high-impact brand exposure.

Pocket Bizz is an all-in-one business management app designed for small to medium enterprises (SMEs), enabling them to streamline daily operations, manage clients, and optimize workflow. The app provides essential tools like invoicing, appointment scheduling, catalog management, and customer relationship tracking—all within a user-friendly, mobile-first interface.

The challenge

Many SMEs struggle with manual processes, disorganized workflows, and lack of access to efficient management tools. The challenge was to create an affordable, intuitive, and scalable solution that simplifies business operations without requiring advanced technical skills.

My solution

I designed a clean and intuitive UI/UX, ensuring ease of use for business owners ith little to no tech experience. The app integrates features like automated invoicing, real-time appointment scheduling, and digital business catalogs, reducing manual work. A dashboard with analytics and financial insights helps users track business performance. Through user testing and iterative design, the app was refined to provide a seamless, efficient experience for SMEs.
